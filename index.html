2018 Travel Guide

Welcome to New York City!

*Population: 8.36 million people. This makes New York City the most populated city in the United States. Land: The city's total land area is approximately 305 square miles. New York City is divided into five boroughs.

*New York City has more people than 39 of the 50 states in the U.S.

*There are about 25,000 restaurants in NYC.

*Brooklyn is the most populated borough in the city (not Manhattan)

*Queens is considered the most ethnically diverse urban area in the world

*New York is composed of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx and Staten Island; each of which is a separate county of New York State.
